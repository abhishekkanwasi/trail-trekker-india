Welcome to Trail Trekker India

Meet Our Founder: Munna Lal Verma

At Trail Trekker India, we’re passionate about adventure and the great outdoors trekking trails. Our journey began four years ago when our founder, Munna Lal Verma, embarked on countless treks across the scenic landscapes of Uttarakhand, India.

From Offline to Online

Trail Trekker India has been well-known in the offline trekking industry for the last 4 years, organizing memorable expeditions for nature enthusiasts. But now, in June-2024 we set our sights on a new horizon: the digital world. With the world increasingly connected online, Trail Trekker India recognizes the immense potential to spread awareness about trekking and its benefits.

Our Mission
  • Promoting Trekking: We believe that trekking isn’t just a physical activity; it’s a transformative experience for an individual. Our online platform aims to inspire more people to explore the breathtaking trails of Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh.
  • Spiritual Yatras: The Char Dham Yatra is a religious expedition that excels in mere physical travel. It becomes a soul-stirring quest—a passage through ancient legends, rugged terrains, and the very essence of devotion. As pilgrims tread the paths to Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath, they not only seek blessings but also each step becomes an exploration of faith, resilience, and self-discovery.
  • Diverse Treks: Uttarakhand offers a diverse range of trekking experiences from snow-capped peaks to lush forests. We curate unique treks, catering to both beginners and seasoned adventurers.
  • Community Building: Trekking is more than just reaching summits; it’s about building connections with fellow trekkers. Join our community and share your passion for the great outdoors.
What we are known for

As an overview, we offer Uttarakhand Yatras and various trails or picturesque treks in Uttarakhand/Himachal Pradesh.
